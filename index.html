Locally Owned & Passionately Independent Since 1987

Jamieson Car & Truck Rental is one of the largest locally owned and operated car and truck rental companies in Kitchener and Woodstock. We’re proud that much of our business comes from repeat customers and referrals. Along with a wide selection of cars, minivans, SUV’s and passenger vans, we have a large truck fleet including 10’ cargo vans, crew cab 4x4 pickup trucks, 16’ cube trucks, 20’ trucks and 24’ straight trucks with power lifts. We work hard to earn your business, and have a solid reputation for integrity, honesty, and reliability.
